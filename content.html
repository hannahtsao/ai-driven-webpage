Revolutionizing Communication

Real-Time Sign Language Translation

By integrating cutting-edge technology into portable headsets, our sign language translation system offers a breakthrough in communicative accessibility.

The system captures hand gestures through the headset's camera, leveraging Google's MediaPipe for precise gesture recognition. This allows for the conversion of sign language into subtitles, enabling real-time translation for individuals with hearing impairments. Our goal is to deliver a seamless experience that fosters understanding and connection.
